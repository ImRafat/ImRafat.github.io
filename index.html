About Us

ICDC is a reliable Islamic Center to follow the Quran and Sunnah. Islamic Center of Delaware County is a non-profit tax-exempt organization that has been formed exclusively for educational, religious, and social purposes. It is registered with the Internal Revenue Service under revenue code 501(c)(3). All of ICDC's activities are in accordance with the teachings of the Quran and following the teachings and traditions of Prophet Muhammad (Peace Be Upon Him). Please donate generously to help us build/maintain this center so that we can spread the knowledge of Islam to the community and provide a location people can come together to learn and grow to become good practicing Muslims. We are located right across from the large shopping center at township line road.
